TruthOS
Mission System Methodology Verdicts Transparency

TruthOS is a verification operating system for factual claims.

It evaluates claims using tiered evidence, explicit logic, probabilistic reasoning, and reversible conclusions. Authority is replaced by process.

No ideology
No persuasion
Evidence-weighted
Audit-ready

Designed For

Courts · Researchers · Journalists · Public Audit

TruthOS does not declare truth.
It exposes evidence, logic, and uncertainty.

How the System Works

1. Claim Intake

Claims are classified, scoped, and structured by entity, time, and variables.

2. Evidence Discovery

Sources are collected across regions and assigned evidence tiers.

3. Logical Evaluation

Contradictions, plausibility, and consistency are tested explicitly.

4. Verdict Assignment

Deterministic rules convert probability into a verdict category.

Evidence Tier Model

Tier-A

Primary documents, official records, raw data

Tier-B

Court records, audits, peer-reviewed studies

Tier-C

Major media (context only)

Tier-D

Social media / anonymous claims (zero trust)

Tier-E

Independent reporters (accuracy-scored)

Verdict Framework

TRUE

≥ 90% probability

LIKELY TRUE

70–89%

MISLEADING

40–69%

UNPROVEN

20–39%

FALSE

< 20%

Transparency & Limitations

  • No verdict is final
  • All logic paths are auditable
  • Unverifiable claims are explicitly labeled
  • Revisions occur when new Tier-A or Tier-B evidence appears
